Just In An Accident?

What to do after a California accident

A few simple steps protect your health — and your claim. When you’re ready, call us for a free review.

1

Get medical care

See a doctor right away, even if you feel okay. Some injuries surface days later, and records protect your claim.

2

Document the scene

Photograph vehicles, injuries, and the location. Get names, insurance, and contact info for everyone involved.

3

Report it

File a police report and notify your insurer — but stick to the facts and don’t admit fault.

4

Don’t accept a quick offer

Early insurance offers are often far below what your claim is worth. Talk to us before you sign anything.

5

Call an accident attorney

We handle the rest — the insurers, the paperwork, the fight — so you can focus on healing. Free, no obligation.

What is my case worth?

California sets strict deadlines, and they can be shorter than you think. Evidence also fades quickly, so it's best to call as soon as possible to protect your rights.
